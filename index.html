The Process

HOW IT WORKS

Four steps to transform consequences into your most powerful motivator

1

Form Group

Gather your accountability squad. Set cycle duration. Build your pact.

• Minimum 2 members

• Cycle: 7-14 days

2

Set Stakes

Add tasks with difficulty levels. Define real consequences that matter.

• Easy / Medium / Hard

• Consequences: Binding

3

Execute

Work with pressure. Document progress. Upload evidence of completion.

• Evidence required

• Maximum pressure

The Origin

How It Started

Monday Night, After a Long Day Message from my project partner. "What's the progress?" We'd been stuck in the same cycle—setting tasks, never finishing them.

Then I had an idea. What if we added REAL stakes?

The Agreement One week. Specific tasks. Whoever didn't finish would reveal their personal journal.

Result: 100% completion rate. Both of us. Tasks that weren't easy.

We were more productive than I could ever remember. We actually managed to get all our tasks done.

— Founder

That's when I knew this had to exist for everyone.
